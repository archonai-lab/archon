# Brainstorming Session

## Phases
- SEED [initiator_only]: 10% — Present the problem space and constraints
- DIVERGE [open_discussion]: 45% — Generate ideas freely — no criticism allowed in this phase
- CONVERGE [open_discussion, proposals]: 35% — Evaluate ideas, propose the strongest candidates, and vote
- PLAN [initiator_only, assignments]: 10% — Define next steps for winning ideas and assign owners

## Rules
- During DIVERGE: no criticism, no "yes but", no feasibility debates — just ideas
- Build on others' ideas with "yes and" instead of replacing them
- Quantity over quality in DIVERGE — filter in CONVERGE
- During CONVERGE: evaluate ideas on impact, feasibility, and alignment with constraints
- Every winning idea must leave with a concrete next step and an owner
