# Incident & Task Triage

## Phases
- PRESENT [initiator_only]: 15% — Present items to triage with initial context
- ASSESS [open_discussion]: 35% — Discuss severity, impact, and scope of each item
- PRIORITIZE [open_discussion, proposals]: 35% — Propose priority order and vote on ranking
- ASSIGN [open_discussion, assignments]: 15% — Assign owners and deadlines for top-priority items

## Rules
- Use severity levels: critical (service down), high (degraded), medium (workaround exists), low (cosmetic)
- Each item must have an owner before the meeting ends
- If an item lacks enough context to triage, mark it "needs investigation" and assign someone to gather info
- Time-box the ASSESS phase — don't deep-dive into solutions, just classify
