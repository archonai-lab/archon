# Code & Design Review

## Phases
- PRESENT [initiator_only]: 20% — Present the artifact or change under review
- ANALYZE [open_discussion]: 50% — Each reviewer examines their domain and shares findings
- REPORT [open_discussion, proposals]: 30% — Consolidate findings into recommendations and vote on actions

## Rules
- Focus on your area of expertise — don't review outside your domain unless you spot something critical
- Cite specific locations (file, function, line) when raising issues
- Classify findings as: blocker, concern, suggestion, or praise
- Proposals should be actionable — "refactor X to Y" not "this could be better"
